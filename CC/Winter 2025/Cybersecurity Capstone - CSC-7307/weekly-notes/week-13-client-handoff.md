The team conducted a formal knowledge transfer session with Industry Mentor at Industry Partner. The session included a live demonstration of the Wazuh deployment, a walkthrough of all deliverables, and handover of the complete documentation package.

### Live Demonstration

- Presented the Industry Partner-specific dashboard with real-time log data flowing from Cisco, MikroTik, and Windows Server sources
- Demonstrated alert triage workflow from initial detection through investigation using the dashboard drill-down panels

#### Deployment Scripts

- Demonstrated the `wazuh_setup.sh` script including its pre-check, backup, and rollback capabilities
