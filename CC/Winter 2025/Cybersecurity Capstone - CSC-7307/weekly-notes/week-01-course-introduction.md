- **Course Structure:** The capstone carries a credit value equivalent to 6 hours per week of dedicated work

- **Expectations:** Teams work with real-world clients on cybersecurity challenges, applying skills from across the program

Students were matched with industry clients for their capstone projects. Our team was assigned to **Industry Partner.**, continuing ISO 27001 compliance work and evaluating SIEM solutions.
