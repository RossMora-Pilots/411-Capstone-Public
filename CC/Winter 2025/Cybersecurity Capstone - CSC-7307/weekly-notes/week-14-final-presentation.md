All capstone teams delivered their final presentations to the class and instructor Course Instructor. The Industry Partner team presented their SIEM deployment project, followed by a group retrospective and course wrap-up discussion.

- Presented a 20-minute overview of the project covering objectives, methodology, technical implementation, and outcomes

### Retrospective

- Team reflected on the 14-week project lifecycle from initial scoping through client delivery
- Identified effective practices: early client engagement, sub-group task division, and iterative script development
- Noted areas for improvement: earlier documentation drafting and more structured internal code reviews
- All team members acknowledged the value of working with a real-world client on production infrastructure
