### About the Organization

The Canadian Cyber Security Network connects cybersecurity professionals with employers across Canada, with a focus on bridging the gap between academic training and industry needs. The organization:

- Works directly with businesses seeking qualified cybersecurity talent
- Helps job seekers find positions aligned with their skills
- Facilitates networking between students, professionals, and employers
- Provides insights into current hiring trends and skill requirements

### Session Highlights

- **Industry Landscape:** Overview of cybersecurity job market in Canada
- **Career Entry Tips:** Practical advice for new graduates entering the field
- **Canada Focus:** Growing cybersecurity needs in various industries, and government sectors
- **Networking Importance:** Building professional connections early in one's career

### Key Takeaways

1. **The skills gap is real** — Employers struggle to find qualified candidates, making new graduates with hands-on experience highly competitive
2. **Canada is growing** — Mining, government, and telecom sectors in the the region are increasing cybersecurity investment
3. **Networking matters early** — Building professional connections during studies accelerates career entry
4. **Practical skills differentiate** — Hands-on experience (like capstone projects) carries more weight than certifications alone in interviews

- Leverage capstone portfolio when applying through CCSN's employer network
