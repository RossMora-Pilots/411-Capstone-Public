## 📋 Course Overview

The Cybersecurity Capstone (CSC-7307) is the culminating course of Cambrian College's Postgraduate Certificate in Cybersecurity. Students work in teams on **real-world client engagements**, applying the full breadth of skills developed throughout the program.

### Capstone Project: Industry Partner — SIEM Deployment & ISO 27001

Our team of 7 was engaged by **Industry Partner.**, a company based in Canada, to:

1. **Continue the ISO 27001:2022 certification journey** started by the Fall 2024 capstone group
2. **Evaluate and deploy SIEM/logging tools** (Wazuh) for the organization
3. **Integrate multi-vendor network devices** (Cisco IOSv, MikroTik) with centralized log management
4. **Build and document a virtual lab environment** using Hyper-V and GNS3

## 🛠️ Skills Demonstrated

- **SIEM Deployment & Configuration** — Wazuh Manager installation, agent deployment, syslog integration
- **Log Management & Analysis** — Syslog forwarding, JSON log pipelines, custom decoders
- **Network Device Integration** — Cisco IOSv and MikroTik log forwarding to Wazuh
- **ISO 27001:2022 Compliance** — Gap analysis, operations security policy development
- **Virtualization** — Hyper-V management, GNS3 network emulation, VM snapshot strategies
- **Bash Scripting & Automation** — Wazuh setup scripts with pre-checks, validation, and rollback
- **Version Management** — Wazuh version locking (yum-plugin-versionlock) for stability
- **Client Communication** — Technical reporting, stakeholder updates, knowledge transfer
